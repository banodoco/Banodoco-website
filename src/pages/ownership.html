Community Ownership Model

Building a company owned by the open source AI art ecosystem

Our Vision for Ownership

At Banodoco, we believe that a company built on the open source ecosystem should be owned by the community that built it. That's why we're sharing 100% of our company's ownership (aside from investor dilution) with people who help the open source ecosystem thrive.

Key Principles

  • Community ownership drives alignment between the company and ecosystem
  • Contributors should be rewarded for their impact
  • Open source collaboration creates more value than closed development

How It Works

Contribution

Contributors to the open source AI art ecosystem can earn ownership through various forms of contribution:

  • Code contributions
  • Documentation
  • Community support
  • Art creation and sharing
  • Educational content

Distribution

Ownership is distributed based on:

  • Impact of contributions
  • Consistency of involvement
  • Community recognition
  • Long-term commitment

Benefits

Community owners receive:

  • Voting rights on key decisions
  • Share in company success
  • Early access to new features
  • Direct influence on product direction

Get Involved

  1. Join our Discord community
  2. Contribute to open source AI art projects
  3. Share your work and help others
  4. Participate in community discussions
